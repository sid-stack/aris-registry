## Program Structure

### Who It's For
- GovCon consultants and BD advisors
- Proposal managers / writers (freelance)
- Grant writers who cross into government contracts
- SAM.gov registration services
- Small business contracting attorneys
- Anyone with a network of government contractors

### How It Works
1. You refer a company to BidSmith
2. They book a demo and convert to a paying customer
3. You earn a cash commission — automatically

### Payout Structure

| Tier | Condition | Commission |
|------|-----------|------------|
| Standard | Any paying referral | 20% of first 3 months |
| Partner | 3+ referrals/month | 25% of first 6 months |
| Strategic | Ongoing pipeline | Custom — revenue share |

*Minimum payout: $25. Payments via PayPal or ACH within 30 days of customer payment clearing.*

## Referral Tracking Setup

**Recommended tool:** Rewardful (integrates with Stripe, ~$49/month) OR PartnerStack (higher tier)

**Simple manual version (free to start):**
- Create unique UTM links per referrer: bidsmith.pro?ref=[name]
- Track in a Google Sheet: Referrer | Link | Clicks | Demos | Conversions | Payout
- Pay manually via PayPal when conversion confirmed

**Upgrade to Rewardful when:** you have 5+ active referral partners
