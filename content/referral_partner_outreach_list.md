# Referral Partner Outreach — 10 Target Contacts
**Week 1 Goal:** Send 10 outreach emails to GovCon-adjacent consultants

Use the email template from referral_program_earn.md.

## Where to Find Them

### LinkedIn Search Queries (run these now)

1. `"GovCon consultant" "business development"` → 2nd connections preferred
2. `"government contracting advisor" "small business"`
3. `"proposal writer" "freelance" "federal"`
4. `"SAM.gov" "registration" "consultant"`
5. `"8(a) certification" "consultant"`
6. `"capture manager" "consultant"`
7. `"federal BD" "advisor" OR "consultant"`

### Other Sources

- **Upwork:** Search "government proposal writing" — freelancers with 10+ reviews
- **GovCon Wire directory:** govconwire.com lists consultants
- **National Contract Management Association (NCMA):** member directory
- **APMP (Association of Proposal Management Professionals):** apmp.org/find-a-member
