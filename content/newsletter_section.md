## Setup Instructions

### Recommended Tool: Beehiiv (free up to 2,500 subscribers)
Alternative: ConvertKit, Mailchimp

**Steps to go live:**
1. Create account at beehiiv.com
2. Set newsletter name: "The Bid Brief by BidSmith"
3. Set sending domain: newsletter@bidsmith.pro (configure DNS in Spacemail)
4. Create signup landing page: bidsmith.pro/newsletter
5. Embed signup form on bidsmith.pro homepage (above fold or exit intent)
6. Set up welcome email (copy below)

## Growth Strategy for Newsletter

**Goal:** 500 subscribers in 90 days

- Add signup CTA to every cold email P.S.: *"Also: subscribe to The Bid Brief for free weekly GovCon intel → bidsmith.pro/newsletter"*
- Post LinkedIn content driving to newsletter (1 post/month specifically about The Bid Brief)
- Add newsletter link in email signature
- Cross-promote in GovCon communities and Reddit
- Anyone who subscribes but doesn't book a demo → nurture via newsletter for 4–6 weeks → convert
