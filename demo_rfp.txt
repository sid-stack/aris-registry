SECTION L: INSTRUCTIONS TO OFFERORS
1. General
The Defense Health Agency (DHA) seeks a solution for the Video Imaging Archive.

2. Security Requirements
L.2.1 Authority to Operate (ATO). The contractor shall provide proof of an existing Authority to Operate (ATO) at IL4 or higher. Proof of ATO must be included in Volume I.
L.2.2 SPRS Score. The contractor must have an active NIST SP 800-171 DoD Assessment in SPRS.

3. Submission
L.3.1 Proposals must be submitted via the PIEE portal no later than May 20, 2024, 12:00 PM EST.

SECTION M: EVALUATION FACTORS
1. Basis of Award
The Government will award a contract resulting from this solicitation to the responsible offeror whose proposal represents the best value.

2. Technical Factors
M.2.1 The Government will evaluate the offeror's approach to achieving the required Authority to Operate (ATO).
M.2.2 Past Performance will be evaluated based on at least three (3) references for similar healthcare IT projects over $5M in the past five years.
