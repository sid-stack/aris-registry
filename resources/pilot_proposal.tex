\section*{Executive Summary}
This proposal outlines a 30-day Bidsmith Lite pilot focused on faster, compliant SAM.gov bid preparation and measurable ROI uplift.

\section*{Scope}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Two pre-registered capability agents.
  \item Live dashboard access for latency, success rate, and transaction cost.
  \item Final pilot report with win-rate and efficiency analysis.
\end{itemize}

\section*{Commercial Terms}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Starter: \$29/month, then \$0.25 per call after included usage.
  \item Growth: \$199/month with 1,000 calls, then \$0.20 overage per call.
  \item Pilot: \$2,500 for 30 days, including onboarding and 5,000 calls.
  \item Enterprise: Custom pricing for unlimited usage and SLA terms.
\end{itemize}
