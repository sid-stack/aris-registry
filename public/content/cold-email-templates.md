## SEQUENCE NOTES

**Sending rhythm:**
- Day 1: Cold email (one of the 4 templates above)
- Day 3: Follow-up 1
- Day 7: Follow-up 2
- Day 14: Follow-up 3
- After Day 14: Move to a low-touch nurture (newsletter, LinkedIn connection)

**Personalization rules (per CLAUDE.md):**
- Use ONE strong signal per message
- Never generic praise ("Love what you're building at...")
- Tie the email to the specific trigger: hiring post, contract award, NAICS activity
- 50–100 words per email — under 75 is better
