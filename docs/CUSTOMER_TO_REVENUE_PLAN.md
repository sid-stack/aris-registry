# Customer → Revenue plan (BidSmith)

**Product:** AI-assisted GovCon workflow — fast first pass on solicitations (requirements / risks / **bid–no-bid recommendation with rationale**).  
**Primary CTA:** **Start free audit** → value → **pay** (free tier **rate-limited per IP** / account as implemented).  
**North-star validation question:** *Will small GovCon teams repeat this every time a new notice drops?*

---

## 1. Ideal ICP (v1 — say no to everything else)

**Who:**  
- **Small firms** (roughly **10–150** employees) selling **B2G / RFP-driven** services or products.  
- **Early or new** to disciplined capture: part-time BD, founder-led sales, or 1–2 capture people—not yet a Deltek-fortress enterprise.

**Who not (anti-ICP for now):**  
- Large integrators with internal shred farms (long cycles, RFPs to *you*).  
- Pure product companies with no RFP motion.  
- Organizations that require on-prem only / no third-party AI (note for enterprise track later).

**Jobs-to-be-done:**  
- “Is this worth our week?” → **bid/no-bid + reasons.**  
- “What will burn us?” → **disqualifiers / heavy reqs surfaced early.**  
- “Can we align the team fast?” → **exportable matrix / artifact.**

---

## 2. Honest value proposition (legal + brand safe)

**We do *not* claim:** “You will win this contract.”  
**We do claim:**  
- **Faster structured understanding** of the solicitation.  
- **Clearer go/no-go framing** with **evidence-linked rationale** (human still decides).  
- **Less time** in PDF archaeology for **small teams**.

**One-liner (external):**  
> **BidSmith is decision support for federal pursuits—structured compliance read, risk flags, and a bid/no-bid recommendation in minutes, not days.**

**Proof language:** Use **sample outputs**, **method blurbs** (how the engine works), **anonymized composites**, **security posture**—not win-rate promises you can’t audit.

---

## 3. Funnel: Customer → Revenue

| Stage | Goal | Primary asset | Metric |
|-------|------|-----------------|--------|
| **Awareness** | Right people learn you exist | LinkedIn + Reddit/communities (value posts, not spam), SEO BOFU pages | Qualified visits, branded search |
| **Trust** | “Serious GovCon tool, not toy” | Security page, founder story, sample audit, clear limits of AI | Time on site, doc clicks |
| **Activation** | **Start free audit** | Landing hero + SAM bar + product | Audit starts / week |
| **Aha** | They see artifact worth sharing internally | Matrix + rationale export | Return within 7 days |
| **Revenue** | Pay for volume / depth | Pricing, in-app upgrade, follow-up email | Free → paid conversion, expansion |

**Rate limit (IP):** Frame as **fair use + abuse protection** in UI copy (“Free tier: X audits / month per account”)—not as punishment. Pair with **clear upgrade path** at moment of value (export, second audit, team).

---

## 4. Credibility stack (GovCon-specific)

Order of operations for a **new** vendor:

1. **Transparency** — what the product does / does not do.  
2. **Artifacts** — downloadable or shareable output.  
3. **Security** — honest architecture (you already lean zero-knowledge / transient—keep that visible).  
4. **Humans** — founder reachable, Calendly for “walk me through output.”  
5. **Social proof over time** — named customers when contract allows; until then, composites + “how we tested.”

**LinkedIn + cold email:**  
- One **specific** signal per touch (hiring, recompete, NAICS).  
- Never oversell AI; **lead with time saved + risk surfaced**.  
- Offer **sample output** or “reply with a notice ID” micro-CTA when appropriate.

---

## 5. Product validation (cheap experiments)

| Experiment | Method | Success signal |
|------------|--------|------------------|
| **Repeat use** | Cohort: users with 2+ audits in 14 days | > X% |
| **Would pay** | In-app 3-question micro-survey after 1st audit | % “very disappointed” if taken away |
| **Artifact quality** | 10 blind comparisons: your summary vs human skim | PMF directionally |
| **ICP fit** | Tag signups by company size in signup or manual | % in ICP band |

Reddit / groups validation you did = **demand signal**; next step is **willingness to pay** and **repeat behavior**.

## 6. Messaging guardrails (GTM + product)

- Replace “win more” **where it implies a guarantee** with **“decide faster” / “fewer bad pursuits” / “shared starting point.”**  
- Win probability in-product = **model estimate**, always paired with **“human judgment required”** microcopy where shown.

---

## 7. Weekly operating rhythm (30 min / Monday)

1. **Funnel numbers:** visits → audits started → signups → paid.  
2. **Top 10 objections** from email/LI (feed copy backlog).  
3. **Two ship items:** one product polish, one marketing asset.
