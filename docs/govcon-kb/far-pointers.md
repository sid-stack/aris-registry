# FAR / DFARS — how to use BidSmith + primary sources

- **FAR** (Federal Acquisition Regulation) and **DFARS** (Defense FAR Supplement) govern how agencies buy. Clause numbers like **52.204-7** (SAM registration) appear constantly in solicitations.
- BidSmith highlights clause-driven risk; for legal interpretation or protest strategy, confirm text in the **official FAR/DFARS** and the solicitation’s **Section I** (contract clauses).
- Common themes: representations and certifications, small business set-asides, cybersecurity (e.g. CMMC/FAR 52.204-21 family), and flow-downs to subcontractors.
