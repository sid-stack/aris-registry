# Opportunity notice types (high level)

- **Solicitation / RFP / RFQ**: Formal request for offers; follow Section L (instructions) and Section M (evaluation) carefully.
- **Sources sought / RFI**: Market research; often shapes later solicitations; still read for teaming and timing signals.
- **Presolicitation / combined synopsis**: Early visibility; use for capture planning before the final RFP drops.
- Dates on SAM.gov (response due, Q&A close) override informal notes—confirm on the notice.
