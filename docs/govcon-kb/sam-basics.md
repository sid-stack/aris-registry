# SAM.gov and vendor identity (summary)

- **SAM.gov** (System for Award Management) is the U.S. federal vendor registry and a primary portal for contract opportunities and related notices.
- **UEI** (Unique Entity ID) replaced DUNS for entity identification. Primes and subcontractors typically need an active SAM registration with a valid UEI to do business with the federal government.
- Registrations must stay **active**; lapsed registration is a common eligibility issue in solicitations.
- Official reference: [SAM.gov](https://sam.gov) — always verify registration and opportunity details at the source.
