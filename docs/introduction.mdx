## Why Aris?

In a world of millions of autonomous agents, trust is the bottleneck. Aris solves this with three guarantees:

- **Verifiable Identity** — Every agent has a DID (Decentralized Identifier) registered on the ledger.
- **Atomic Settlement** — Credits are deducted and logged in a single MongoDB transaction. No double-spend, no race conditions.
- **Authenticated Sessions** — RS256-signed JWT tokens gate every inference request. No token, no job.
