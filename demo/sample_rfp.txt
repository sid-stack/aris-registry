SECTION L — INSTRUCTIONS TO OFFERORS

L.1 ELIGIBILITY
All offerors shall be registered in SAM.gov at time of proposal submission and must maintain active registration through award.

L.2 TECHNICAL VOLUME REQUIREMENTS
Offerors shall demonstrate a minimum of five (5) years of experience providing IT infrastructure support services to Department of Defense components.

Offerors shall provide three (3) past performance references. Each reference must represent a contract valued at $5,000,000 or greater, completed within the past five (5) years.

Offerors shall submit documentation confirming CMMC Level 2 certification status per DFARS 252.204-7021. Proposals without CMMC documentation will be deemed non-responsive.

L.3 PERSONNEL REQUIREMENTS
Key Personnel must hold active Secret security clearances. No interim clearances will be accepted. Personnel must be available for on-site performance at Walla Walla, WA no later than Day 1 of contract performance.

L.4 CYBERSECURITY
Offerors shall have a current NIST SP 800-171 self-assessment posted to the Supplier Performance Risk System (SPRS) at the time of proposal submission. DFARS 252.204-7012 applies to all data processed under this contract.

SECTION M — EVALUATION CRITERIA

M.1 BASIS FOR AWARD
Award will be made on a Best Value basis. Technical Approach is the most important evaluation factor. Past Performance is the second most important. Price is least important.

M.2 TECHNICAL EVALUATION
Technical Approach shall be evaluated on:
- Cloud migration methodology and zero-downtime transition planning
- CMMC Level 2 documented posture
- Network operations experience at DoD facilities

M.3 PAST PERFORMANCE
Past performance shall be evaluated for relevance, recency, and quality. References with direct Army experience shall be rated more favorably.

SECTION H — SPECIAL CONTRACT REQUIREMENTS
H.1 SUBCONTRACTING PLAN: Small Business set-aside applies per FAR 52.219-6.
H.4 BID BOND: Not required per this solicitation.
